About Us

Karibu Nakuru,
Karibu Familia!

We are a small family-owned restaurant right in the heart of Nakuru that has grown into a place where locals and visitors come together to enjoy authentic Kenyan meals made with love and fresh ingredients from our own backyard and local farmers.

No fancy stuff. Just honest, delicious food that reminds you why Kenyan cuisine is world-class. We cook slow, so you can eat up and leave with a full belly and a happy heart.
